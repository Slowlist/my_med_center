# My Medical Center

My Medical Center is a realistic hospital management game focused on the business and operational side of running a large hospital. Instead of only focusing on direct patient care, the game includes systems such as budgeting, grants, contracts, room placement, employee traits, research upgrades, technology systems, and hospital growth.

## Project Concept

The goal of the game is to build and manage a modern medical center while balancing money, staff needs, patient flow, technology, and long term planning. Players can improve the hospital through research branches such as advanced tools, automation, filing systems, tablets, digital backups, servers, Wi Fi, and cable infrastructure.

## What Codex Helped With

I used Codex to help plan and build parts of the game, including:

- Grant and contract ideas
- Employee trait systems
- Tutorial improvements
- Research and technology branches
- Hospital department planning
- Gameplay systems and structure
- UI and prototype support

## Current Status

This project is currently in prototype/design development. The repository includes game files, concept notes, screenshots, and planning material for the Codex Creator Challenge.
